Lesson Recap

Lesson Recap

Maintaining high performing cloud infrastructure requires training, time, and data.Cloud providers are always making performance improvements to their services and offering incentives to make periodic upgrades attractive to their customers.

In order to achieve optimal performance, you need the data to support your service and configuration choices. Taking the time to monitor your cloud workloads will reward you with performance optimized systems.
